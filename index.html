Our Vision

The idea to create this foundation is to develop a software which allows us to run all programming languages in low end PC's and open communities in every city so that tech enthusiast should learn something new and innovative.

Our Mission

Script Foundation's mission is to promote the industry skills through which tech enthusiast are and should have a clarity and move ahead to make something unique for this world.
